B: KPI Cards + Full-Width Feed

Sales KPI cards across the top, then a full-width activity feed below. Tasks live in the "Tasks" tab rather than the sidebar. Cleaner, more focused.

Both options include:

  • 3 tabs: Following (records you follow) · All Activity (team-wide) · Tasks (your to-dos)
  • Activity type filter: Calls, Emails, Notes, Meetings, Deal changes
  • User filter: Filter feed by team member
  • Log Activity button: Quick-add notes, calls, meetings from the feed
  • Clickable links: Contact/company/deal names link to their detail pages
  • Relative timestamps: "2m ago", "1h ago", "yesterday"
